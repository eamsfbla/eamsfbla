Winter 2023 Newsletter!

  • Aaditya Mittal & Naisha Bhandari
  • December 19th, 2023

Hey MCA FBLA!

As we embrace the winter season, we are delighted to share some exciting updates with you.

First and foremost, a heartfelt thank you goes out to all our dedicated members who took the time to write letters for the Advocate 4 Anthony initiative. Your letters have been printed and personally delivered to senior citizens, spreading warmth and joy throughout our community.

We recently appointed a Community Service Committee as well. Congratulations to Ishan Nagpal, Manya Elugoti, Avika Shukla, Alexander Poon. The Community Service Committee is led by the Community Service Project team, Srivarun Kankanala, Rahil Patel, and Isaac Chan. Additionally, thanks to everyone who supported our Samosa Chaat Fundraiser this week! Proceeds will go towards cutting conference costs, as well as the chapter Community Service Project.

Congratulations to the 5 chapter members who were selected for NJ FBLA state committees. Naisha Bhandari and Shreya Iyer were selected for the Social Media Committee, led by State Historian Alexandra Li. Ethan Poon and Rahil Patel were selected for the Technology committee, led by State Webmaster Aarav Mulinti. Aditya Kirubakaran was selected for the Community Service Committee, led by State VP of Community Service, Anika Dugal. Finally, congratulations to everyone who placed at regionals! Make sure to check out the Google Classroom post or the Website Blog post with all the winners listed.

Your MCA FBLA Webmasters,
Ajay P, Naisha B, and Aaditya M
