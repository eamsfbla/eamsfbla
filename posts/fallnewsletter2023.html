Fall 2023 Newsletter!

  • Aaditya Mittal & Ajay Parthiba
  • September 10th, 2023

Hey MCA FBLA!

As the school year starts, we have a few more weeks left until membership sign-ups are due! You must fill out this form by September 14th before paying the $30 dues. An important note: Dues paid after September 14 will be charged the late dues rate of $35 (not applicable to incoming freshman). Membership will be capped at 100 members so be sure to complete the form and pay dues ASAP.

Get ready for an incredible fall season filled with growth, learning, and connections! We're thrilled to announce two upcoming conferences that you won't want to miss: The State Fall Leadership Conference (SFLC) and the National Fall Leadership Conference (NFLC). Whether you're looking to refine your business skills, network with fellow FBLA members, or explore new horizons, these events have something special in store for you.

SFLC will be held at Kean University, Hillside, NJ on October 24th, 2023from 9:00 am - 1:30 pm. For 25$ (includes a light breakfast + lunch) you will get to meet other NJ FBLA members, make new friends, and also meet and get to know the new NJ FBLA state officers. There are also several engaging workshops held and fun activities. This will count as an EXCUSED absence from school. Transportation is covered.

NFLC will be held at the Rhode Island Convention Center in Providence, RI from November 9th - November 11th, 2023 (during November break). Depending on interest and transportation costs attending NFLC will cost anywhere from $250-$350 and you will get to meet many other FBLA members from different states, make a lot of new friends across the nation, and get acquainted with the National FBLA officer team. NFLC also has many workshops and learning opportunities so you can maximize your high school FBLA experience.

Check the MCA FBLA google classroom for additional information and flyers. Complete this form if you are interested in attending SFLC or NFLC.

Your MCA FBLA Webmasters,
Ajay P, Naisha B, and Aaditya M
